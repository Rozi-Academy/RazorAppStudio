Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 6 December, 2024

Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we use the permissions requested by the application to deliver its functionality while safeguarding your data.

1. Permissions and Their Usage

We request certain permissions to provide the app's features effectively:

1.1 Calendar Permissions

  • READ_CALENDAR, WRITE_CALENDAR: Used to view, add, edit, or sync calendar events.

1.2 Alarm and Notification Permissions

  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM, POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Enable timely task reminders, alarms, and notifications for app-related events.

1.3 Microphone Permission

  • RECORD_AUDIO: For voice input, voice commands, or audio-related functionality.

1.4 Camera Permission

  • CAMERA: For capturing photos or videos, if required by app functionality.

1.5 Storage Permissions

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO: Access, read, or store media files and user-generated content.

1.6 Network Permissions

  • INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Provide internet access for data syncing, online services, or other connectivity-based features.

1.7 System Permissions

  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Ensure scheduled tasks or notifications function after your device restarts.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: Display floating alerts or overlays for enhanced usability.

1.8 Foreground Service Permissions

  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PROJECTION, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE, FOREGROUND_SERVICE_CAMERA: Enable critical background services such as ongoing task tracking or notifications.

2. How We Use Your Information

The permissions mentioned above are used solely to provide the intended features of the application, including:

  • Scheduling tasks and sending reminders.
  • Capturing and processing audio or video as per user requests.
  • Storing and managing files or media.
  • Synchronizing calendar events and tasks.
  • Maintaining app performance and functionality.

3. Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data against unauthorized access or misuse. Sensitive permissions are only activated when required, and data is stored securely on your device or encrypted in databases.

4. User Control

You have full control over the permissions granted to the application. You can manage or revoke permissions at any time through your device's settings. Note that disabling certain permissions may limit app functionality.

5. Third-Party Services

The application may integrate with third-party services, such as Google Calendar, for syncing events. Please review their privacy policies to understand how your data is managed by these services.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in permissions or functionality. Updated policies will be provided within the application or on the relevant website.
